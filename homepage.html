Welcome to the university of kelaniya


The university of kelaniya is committed to provide high quality education and to conduct high impact research which will contribute significantly to the enhancement of exiting knowledge in various fields of Humanities,Medicines,Science,Social Sciences,Commerce & Management and to the development of the country.

Vision
 To become a central of excellence in creation and dissemination of knowledge of sustainable development.

Mission
 To narture intellectual citizens through creativity and innovation,who contribute to the national development.
